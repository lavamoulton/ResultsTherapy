Insurance

I bill all commercial insurance plans as an out of network provider as a free courtesy to clients. Most insurance plans with out-of-network benefits will reimburse you 60-90% of my fee when you submit a claim after the deductible is met. Please note that I make no guarantees about the level of reimbursement from your insurance company and clients are responsible to be informed about their individual benefits.

Questions to ask insurance for out-of-network reimbursement:

  • Do I have out-of-network benefits for counseling services with a Licensed Professional Counselor for procedure code 90837 or 90834?
  • What portion of the amount paid will I be reimbursed?
  • Do I have a deductible that must be met before insurance will reimburse for my out-of-network sessions?
  • What is the procedure for obtaining reimbursement?
  • Does my plan reimburse for telehealth (online) sessions?
